Nasi Krawu

Nasi Krawu

Nasi Krawu is a traditional dish from Gresik, served with rice, shredded beef, spiced coconut, and chili sauce on a banana leaf. Its rich blend of savory, sweet, and spicy flavors makes it a local favorite.

Lontong Roomo

Lontong Roomo

Lontong Roomo is a signature dish from Gresik, made of rice cakes served with savory peanut sauce, fresh vegetables, and crispy crackers. Its light yet flavorful taste makes it a popular traditional delicacy.

Otak-Otak Bandeng

Otak-Otak Bandeng

Otak-otak Bandeng is a famous Gresik delicacy made from milkfish that is deboned, seasoned with rich spices, stuffed back into its skin, and then grilled. It offers a smoky aroma and a unique blend of savory and slightly sweet flavors.

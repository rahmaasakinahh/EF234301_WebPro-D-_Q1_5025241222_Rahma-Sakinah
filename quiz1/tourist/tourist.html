Sekapuk Chalk Hill

Located in East Java, Sekapuk Chalk Hill is a stunning landscape of towering white limestone cliffs that contrast beautifully with the blue sky. Perfect for photography and exploration, this hidden gem combines natural beauty with a touch of history for an unforgettable experience.

Tomb of Sunan Giri

A sacred landmark in Gresik, the Tomb of Sunan Giri is not only a spiritual destination but also a cultural treasure. As one of the revered Wali Songo, his resting place attracts pilgrims and travelers alike, offering a serene atmosphere, rich history, and timeless legacy of Islam in Java.

City of Industry❕City of Santri❕

This is "Gresik"

I was born and raised in Gresik, a city often called both the City of Santri 🕌 and the City of Industry 🏭. Sure, there are plenty of factories here, but the atmosphere still feels warm and grounded in strong religious life.

What’s funny is, even though Gresik is literally right next to Surabaya, many people still don’t really know about it 😅. But honestly, Gresik has its own story—a mix of industrial hustle and the rich traditions of its santri community.

For me, Gresik isn’t just a place to live, it’s where I truly found the meaning of a warm “home” 🏡✨.
